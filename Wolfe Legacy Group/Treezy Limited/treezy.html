Implementation Guidelines

As a rebellious, inclusive brand targeting digital natives, Who Treezy's implementation must be bold, authentic, and socially engaging while maintaining street credibility and community trust.

Visual Consistency

  • Bold use of hot pink and neon green for maximum social media impact
  • Question mark prominently featured across all brand touchpoints
  • High contrast design elements optimized for mobile and social platforms
  • Consistent typography hierarchy with Oswald for impact, Poppins for readability
  • Street photography aesthetics that feel authentic and unpolished

Social-First Strategy

  • Content created mobile-first for vertical viewing on Stories and TikTok
  • Product photography optimized for social sharing and discovery
  • User-generated content encouraged and amplified across all platforms
  • Community engagement prioritized over traditional advertising
  • Real-time trend participation while maintaining brand authenticity

Community Building

  • Customer purchase stories featured prominently across social channels
  • Inclusive messaging that welcomes all styles, budgets, and backgrounds
  • Transparency in sourcing, authentication, and pricing processes
  • Collaborative content with community members and micro-influencers
  • Question-driven campaigns that encourage participation and sharing

Brand Evolution

  • Flexibility to adapt visual elements while maintaining core question mark identity
  • Seasonal campaign variations that keep content fresh and engaging
  • Platform-specific optimizations while maintaining brand consistency
  • Growth preparation for potential expansion beyond apparel (Treezy Limited)
  • Continuous community feedback integration into brand development
