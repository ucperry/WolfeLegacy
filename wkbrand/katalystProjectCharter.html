Project Overview

Purpose: To implement a comprehensive healthy workplace program that promotes physical activity through step tracking, improves nutrition through healthy lunch options, and supports mental wellness through awareness initiatives. This evidence-based program aims to improve employee health outcomes, reduce healthcare costs, and enhance workplace productivity and engagement.

Business Case

Current organizational health metrics indicate opportunities for improvement in employee wellness, with 68% of employees reporting high stress levels, 45% indicating poor nutrition habits, and only 32% meeting recommended daily physical activity guidelines. This program addresses these challenges through a holistic, data-driven approach that aligns with our organization's commitment to employee wellbeing and performance excellence.

Project Scope

In Scope

  • Development and implementation of step tracking technology platform
  • Vendor partnerships and healthy food program implementation
  • Mental health awareness campaign design and execution
  • Employee training and education programs
  • Data collection and analytics framework
  • Physical workspace modifications for wellness activities
  • Program communication and change management

Out of Scope

  • Individual medical consultations or health assessments
  • Major building renovations or construction
  • Long-term healthcare benefit plan modifications
  • Personal fitness equipment purchases for employees
  • Off-site team building or retreat activities
