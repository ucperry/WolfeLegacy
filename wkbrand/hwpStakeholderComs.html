Communication Strategy by Project Phase

Phase 1: Planning & Design (Months 1-2)

Focus: Executive & Core Team
  • Kickoff Meeting: All stakeholders introduction and expectations
  • Weekly Executive Updates: CHRO on progress and decisions needed
  • Design Workshops: Wellness Committee input on program features
  • Technical Planning: IT requirements and security protocols

Phase 2: Infrastructure Setup (Months 3-4)

Focus: Implementation Teams
  • Implementation Meetings: Weekly progress with IT and Facilities
  • Vendor Coordination: Bi-weekly vendor performance reviews
  • Manager Briefings: Prepare middle management for rollout
  • Early Communication: Employee awareness campaign begins

Phase 3: Pilot Program (Month 5)

Focus: Pilot Participants
  • Pilot Participant Training: Hands-on program introduction
  • Daily Check-ins: Immediate feedback collection and issue resolution
  • Executive Updates: Pilot results and adjustments needed
  • Stakeholder Feedback: All parties input on pilot outcomes

Phase 4: Full Implementation (Months 6-7)

Focus: All Employees
  • Launch Event: Organization-wide program introduction
  • Training Sessions: Department-by-department program training
  • Daily Support: Help desk and technical support communications
  • Success Celebrations: Early wins and participation milestones

Phase 5: Evaluation & Optimization (Month 8+)

Focus: Results & Sustainability
  • Results Presentation: Executive dashboard with ROI metrics
  • Employee Surveys: Comprehensive program feedback collection
  • Optimization Planning: Continuous improvement recommendations
  • Sustainability Planning: Long-term program maintenance strategy

Communication Channels & Tools

Executive Communications

CHRO, Senior Leadership
  • Executive Dashboard: Real-time KPIs and budget tracking
  • Monthly Reports: Strategic updates and risk assessments
  • Quarterly Reviews: Comprehensive program evaluation
  • Ad-hoc Briefings: Critical issue escalation and decision support

Project Team Communications

PM, Wellness Committee, Implementation Teams
  • Weekly Status Meetings: Progress updates and issue resolution
  • Project Management Platform: Task tracking and collaboration
  • Shared Documentation: Centralized project resources and updates
  • Instant Messaging: Real-time communication and quick decisions

Employee Communications

All Staff, Participants
  • Company Intranet: Program information and resources
  • Email Newsletters: Regular updates and success stories
  • All-hands Meetings: Major announcements and Q&A sessions
  • Digital Signage: Daily tips and participation reminders

Vendor Communications

Technology, Food, Service Providers
  • Vendor Portals: Performance tracking and issue reporting
  • Monthly Reviews: Service level assessment and improvement planning
  • Contract Management: Formal documentation and change requests
  • Emergency Contacts: 24/7 support for critical issues

Communication Escalation Matrix

Issue Escalation Procedures

Level 1: Operational Issues

Contact: Project Manager

Timeline: Same day resolution

Examples: Technical glitches, vendor delays, participant questions

Level 2: Significant Concerns

Contact: Wellness Committee Chair

Timeline: 48-hour response

Examples: Low participation, budget concerns, stakeholder resistance

Level 3: Critical Issues

Contact: CHRO (Executive Sponsor)

Timeline: Immediate response

Examples: Legal compliance issues, major vendor failures, program cancellation risk
